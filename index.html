Diabetes Myths

Does diabetes happen ONLY because of sugar? No. If you don't eat sugar or sweets, but still eat a lot of unhealthy foods, you can gain too much weight. That can also lead to diabetes.

Is it okay for a diabetic person to eat ghee? Yes. After eating, your blood glucose levels can go up and down too much (that's unhealthy). Eating ghee helps to control that.
